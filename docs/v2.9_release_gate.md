## Summary

This gate records the validation evidence for the v2.9 Evidence-Grounded AI Brief
work as it currently stands on the `v2.9-evidence-grounded-ai-brief` branch. It is

## Safety Boundary Confirmation

Confirmed unchanged on this branch:

- Rule-Based Detector is the detection authority.
- Risk Level and Decision are deterministic (rule plus triage policy).
- `BLOCK`, `MONITOR`, and `ALLOW` are simulated decisions only.
- The Evidence-Grounded AI Brief, RAG, Similar Cases, and Graph context are
  advisory only and do not override the official Risk Level or Decision.
- Similar cases do not prove current compromise or successful execution.
- Graph context is not a detection source.
- No real firewall, WAF, EDR, account, cloud, SIEM, or SOAR action is performed.
- No exploit, proof-of-concept, or traffic generation is produced.
- Human review is required.

This branch remains an academic / portfolio-grade blue-team triage prototype. It
is not a production IDS/IPS and not a red-team tool.

## Known Limitations
